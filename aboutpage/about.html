Our Story

Gorkha Christian Church, established on March 6, 1988, stands as a testament to the enduring faith and community spirit fostered by its founder, Late.Shri Padam Prashad Neupane. This church has become a cornerstone for spiritual growth and fellowship, providing a welcoming environment for worshippers and visitors alike.
Under the leadership of the current pastor, Tika Rai, Gorkha Christian Church continues to thrive, offering various programs and services that cater to the needs of its congregation. The church is dedicated to promoting Christian values and community engagement, making it a vital part of the local landscape.

Statements of beliefs

Belief in One God

The Bible emphasizes monotheism, the belief in a single, all-powerful God who created and sustains the universe. This belief is foundational in Christianity and is articulated in the Shema: “Hear, O Israel: The Lord our God, the Lord is one” (Deuteronomy 6:4).

The Trinity

Christians believe in the Trinity, the concept of one God in three persons: the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit. This belief is rooted in various passages, such as the Great Commission: “Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit” (Matthew 28:19).

Salvation through Jesus Christ

Central to Christian belief is the idea that salvation is granted through faith in Jesus Christ. According to the Bible, Jesus’ death and resurrection provide atonement for sin and the promise of eternal life: “For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life” (John 3:16).

The Ten Commandments

These commandments, given to Moses on Mount Sinai, are a set of ethical guidelines that outline the moral conduct expected of believers. They include directives such as honoring one’s parents, refraining from murder, theft, and adultery (Exodus 20:1-17).

Love and Compassion

The Bible teaches the importance of love and compassion toward others. Jesus summarized the greatest commandments as loving God with all one’s heart and loving one’s neighbor as oneself (Matthew 22:37-39).

Resurrection and Eternal Life

Christians believe in the resurrection of the dead and eternal life. The Bible speaks of a future resurrection where believers will be raised to eternal life with God: “I am the resurrection and the life. Whoever believes in me, though he die, yet shall he live” (John 11:25).

The Holy Spirit

The Bible teaches that the Holy Spirit is present with believers, guiding, comforting, and empowering them. The Holy Spirit is described as a helper who leads believers into all truth (John 14:16-17).

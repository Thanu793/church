Our Ministries

In our church, we prioritize a diverse range of ministries to cater to the spiritual and communal needs of our congregation. Our General Ministries provide a foundation for various outreach and support activities, ensuring that we address the different aspects of our community’s well-being. Sunday School is a vital part of our offerings, nurturing the young minds in our congregation with lessons of faith and values. Additionally, our Youth Service is designed to engage and inspire our younger members, providing them with a platform to grow spiritually and socially. Although Family Ministries was initially part of our plan, we've made adjustments to better align with our current focus areas. Through these activities, we strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for all.And last but not least is our Women Ministry, which is dedicated to nurturing and empowering women in our congregation.

Sunday School

"Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not depart from it" Proverbs 22:6

Sunday School Ministry plays a crucial role in the spiritual growth and education of our church’s youngest members. By providing age-appropriate biblical lessons, activities, and discussions, Sunday School helps children build a strong foundation in their faith. This ministry not only teaches the stories and teachings of the Bible but also encourages children to develop their own personal relationship with God.

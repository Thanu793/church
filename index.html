Welcome to Gorkha Christian Church

Mon Bosti, Karbi Anglong, Assam - 782480

Nestled in a tranquil setting, Gorkha Christian Church stands as a beacon of faith and community. Adorned with beautiful lightning, especially during festive seasons, the church welcomes everyone with open arms.Its warm, inviting atmosphere makes it a perfect place for worship, reflection, and fellowship.
Join us for uplifting services, community events, and activities that foster a sense of belonging. At Gorkha Christian Church, you will find a supportive community striving to grow together in faith, love, and service. We invite you to come and experience a place where spiritual lives flourish and frienships blossom.
